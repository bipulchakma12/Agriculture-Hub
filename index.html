Pest Control

Integrated Pest Management (IPM)
  1. Identify pests and beneficial species accurately.
  2. Monitor regularly (scouting, traps, thresholds).
  3. Use cultural controls: crop rotation, planting date, sanitation.
  4. Introduce or conserve biological control agents (predators, parasitoids).
  5. Apply targeted, least-toxic chemical interventions only when thresholds are exceeded.
